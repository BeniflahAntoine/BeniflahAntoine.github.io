Pong Game

Press "W" and "S" to move the Red Paddle and press the "up arrow key" and "down arrow key" to move the Blue Paddle.

In order to spice things up, press "D" for the Red Paddle and "left arrow key" for the Blue Paddle to speed up the ball!

Warning: The ball can get through the paddles if it gets faster than the speed of light!

Tip: If the ball gets stuck in the paddle, press "D" for the Red Paddle and "left arrow key" for the Blue Paddle, to get the ball off.
